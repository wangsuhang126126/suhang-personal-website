## From generation to coordination

Japan's residential energy market is moving beyond the simple question of how much solar can be installed on a roof.

The next phase is about how homes store, manage, and coordinate energy across a wider system.

## Why storage matters

<Callout type="note">
The next residential energy opportunity is less about isolated hardware and more about coordinated behavior.
</Callout>

Residential batteries change the value of solar in three practical ways:

- They shift daytime generation into evening use.
- They create backup value during outages.
- They make households more flexible participants in the grid.

> Solar creates energy. Storage creates timing.

## What comes next

The important question is no longer only `how many panels`. It is how solar, batteries, home energy management, VPPs, and demand response begin to work together.

For Japan, that shift will depend on product quality, installer trust, policy design, and clear customer education.
